HTML5 Specialist Program

The HTML5 Specialist Program is designed to provide developers with proof of their coding skills. The program includes a course and exam. Once you complete both the course and the exam, you will receive the HTML5 Specialist Designation.

Upon earning the HTML5 Specialist Designation, you can display the HTML Specialist badge onyour resume, portfolio on your linkedin profile. You will also receive a digital certifcate that you can print and frame.
